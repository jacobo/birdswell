About Us

BirdSwell is surf and nature inspired wearable art. It is a combination of passion for art, design, surfing and nature. Every BirdSwell product is created to spread and bring love, joy, light.

We are independently owned and operated by surfers. BirdSwell was founded at the end of 2009 and re-ignited in 2021.

All designs are original creations by artist and surfer, Julie-Ann Burkhart (J-Bird).

The printing will be done with the linoleum (lino) block printing process. J-Bird has decided to do lino block printing because it allows her to handcraft the products from start to finish. She absolutely love the lino block process and carving into the block. The quality of the printing is beautifully imperfect creating a vintage look and feel.

Clothing and Specialty Products will be limited edition and available for a limited amount of time. After 2 weeks from a product release date, we will close sales and the design will be discontinued.
