From 2009 – 2014, I created and ran BirdSwell. In 2014 and 2017, my kids, Leo and Adeline were born and my focus changed. It has been around 7 years since I have created for BirdSwell.

A month ago while I was sitting at my mediation spot in Pacifica, CA, I realized that BirdSwell is still my passion which is a combination of surfing, ocean, nature, and art creation. These are the things that bring me joy! I want my art creations to spread and bring love, joy, and light. BirdSwell is my artistic voice and my soul’s offering.

However, BirdSwell will be done in a different capacity:
1. The printing will be done with the linoleum (lino) block printing process. I have decided to do lino block printing instead of screen printing because it allows me to handcraft the products from start to finish. I absolutely love the lino block process and carving into the block. The quality of the printing is beautifully imperfect creating a vintage look and feel.

2. Clothing and Specialty Products will be limited edition and available for a limited amount of time. After 2 weeks from the release date, I will close sales and the design will be discontinued.

3. Lino Block BirdSwell Prints and Greeting Cards of my paintings will be readily available.

I have always loved doing BirdSwell and I am excited to reignite this passion and share my offering with you all once again.

Spread the Swell,
J-Bird
